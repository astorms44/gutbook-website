Gutbook

A private IBS & FODMAP tracker for iPhone and Apple Watch.

Privacy Policy Support

Gutbook helps you log meals, symptoms, medications, water, stress, sleep, and menstrual cycle, then surfaces the food and lifestyle patterns most correlated with how you feel. Built around the Monash low-FODMAP framework and Bristol stool scale.

All data stays in your private iCloud. No analytics. No ads. No accounts.
